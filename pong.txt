Pong game  by Thomas George

assembler state machine has:
startup ... w/pongstr_screen()
instructions ... w/ instr_screen()
play ... w/ play()
gameover ... w/winner_screen()
tilt ... w/ tilt_screen()

Tilt is a game interupting function controlled by the lower button off P1.3. It will interupt the game.
